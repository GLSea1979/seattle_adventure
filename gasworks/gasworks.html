Username, welcome to Gas Works Park! What should we do here?

Arches

Let's run under the concrete arches! They are old train trestles from the Seattle gas light company.

Kite Hill

Let’s walk up the winding path to the top of the Kite Hill. The breeze from Lake Union makes today the perfect day for flying kites in the park.

Sundial

Let’s visit sundial at the top of Kite Hill. The sundial is made out of bronze, shells, and glass. We can use our shadow to tell the time of the day.
