Username, welcome to the Ballard Locks! Where do you want to see while you are here?

Spillway

The spillway helps maintain the water level of the ship canal and lakes. Let’s watch the water flow into the Sound.

Salmon

There are many types of salmon that migrate up the fish ladder every season. Let’s see if we can identify any Chinook, Coho, Sockeye or Steelhead salmon.

Fish Ladder

The fish ladder helps salmon swim upstream to freshwater. Let’s go to the viewing room!
