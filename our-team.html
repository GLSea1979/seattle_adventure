Meet Our Team

Collin Smith

Collin grew up in Eastern Washington working on the family orchard while in school before going to Wenatchee Valley College, Washington State University and Central Washington University wasting 6 years of his life getting degrees. He eventually joined the Army, made selection for 2/75th Ranger Battalion, was stationed in Fort Lewis, and deployed 4 times. After separating from the Army he has started a real estate business and a marijuana producer/processor. All of that worked out so well he has decided to retire and is now at Codefellows learning how to code. Collin's Github page

Shelly Tang

Shelly grew up in Austin, Texas, the land of live music, breakfast tacos, and BBQ. When she is not spending all her time learning to code, she enjoys running, drinking coffee, and exploring Seattle. She wants to write better code and continue to learn how to build things. Shelly's Github page

Gary Lundgren

Gary grew up in Minnesota then ran off to Alaska when he was 18. After many years of living in cold lands he came to Seattle, home of mild weather. He enjoys hiking, biking and the occasional board game. He enjoys building things and is starting to dream in code. Gary's Github page
